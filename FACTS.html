Most times people think about only unemployment has poverty, but poverty is not only about unemployment. Poverty is mainly about not having enough money to meet basic needs including food, clothing and shelter. So many people around the World can’t afford to feed themselves and provide for their family. Around 27-28 percent of all children in developing countries are estimated to have a stunted growth or under weighed. According to UNICEF, 22,000 children die each day due to poverty, and they die quietly in some of the poorest villages on earth, far removed from the scrutiny and the conscience of the world. It is estimated that out of two billion kids Worldwide one billion of them live in poverty.

CAUSES OF POVERTY

There are so many causes of poverty in the World. It could be as a result of:

1. Unemployment:Unemployment can be seen as the act of not having a job. According to Economics, Unemployment occurs when a person who is actively searching for employment is unable to find work. ..

2. Crime & Corruption:Corruption can be defined as an illegal act by the officials of a country for their private gain.eg embezzlement of money, abuse of power etc

3. Homeless:Is the lack of having a roof over a person’s head. This can be seen in most African countries where some citizens are sleeping under bridges.

4. Bad Economy:Is a situation whereby a country’s economy rate or growth is low . Most underdeveloped countries around the world are suffering from a bad economy.

5. Poor Education:Is the lack of standard education. Most citizens in some does not have a good education through lack of money. You find them in streets selling goods instead of going to school.

6. Political Instability:Is a situation whereby a country’s political government cease to work as it should. The cause of this is the greediness of government officials and this affects citizens of the country a lot.
